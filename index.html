QC Checklist: Geophysical Processing

Use this tool to complete quality control checklists for various geophysical survey platforms including MBES (Multibeam Echosounder) on vessels and AUVs, SBP (Sub-Bottom Profiler) systems, and SSS (Side Scan Sonar) configurations.

Available Checklists

MBES Vessel: Multibeam Echosounder on crewed vessel or USV
MBES AUV: Multibeam Echosounder on Autonomous Underwater Vehicle
SBP Vessel: Sub-Bottom Profiler on vessel platform
SBP AUV: Sub-Bottom Profiler on AUV platform
SSS Towed: Side Scan Sonar towed configuration
Cross Comparison: Multi-source position verification

How to Use

  • Select a checklist from the sidebar
  • Mark each check item as Pass, Fail, or N/A
  • Add notes and sign off with initials and date
  • Use Bulk fill to quickly populate signer and date fields
  • Export/Import your progress as JSON
  • Print the completed checklist for records

Color Key

Pass - Check complete and accepted
Fail - Issue found, corrective action needed
N/A - Not applicable for this survey
